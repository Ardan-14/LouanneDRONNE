HUMUS

Context

Created with a friend, "Humus" is a role-playing game universe that explores a contemporary world where humans develop unique powers. These supernatural abilities have led to the creation of specialized institutions, such as schools for heroes and reporters, each training their students to master their skills and navigate a society transformed by these gifts.

Game System :

The "Humus" system is based on major skills rated from 1 to 9. To succeed in an action, the player must achieve a number of successes on dice that matches the difficulty of the action. Rolls are made with three ten-sided dice, where a result of 0 equals a critical success and a 9 equals a critical failure. A success is defined as a roll equal to or lower than the relevant skill.

Gameplay Experience :

Players take on the roles of heroes, journalists, or simple students, discovering and exploiting their abilities while interacting with the challenges and mysteries of this world. The game's universe, set in a politically active world, offers rich scenarios filled with interactions and moral dilemmas, blending adventure and conspiracy.

Objectifs

This game was designed to offer an intense narrative experience, where every decision is crucial. This RPG is the result of several years of reflection and multiple testing campaigns. The rules and the universe have undergone updates to reach a complete and fully playable version.

Summary
  • Universe – A contemporary world where some humans have powers, leading to the creation of hero schools.
  • Game System – Based on major skills rated from 1 to 9.
  • Dice Rolls – Three ten-sided dice; a success occurs by rolling equal to or lower than the skill. 0 is a critical success, 9 is a critical failure.
